Our Story

A kitchen full of
good things.

Zoë started baking from her mother's kitchen in Soweto at the age of twelve. What began as weekend treats for the family became a full bakery in 2018 — and we haven't stopped since.

Every loaf, tart and celebration cake is made by hand using locally sourced ingredients. No shortcuts. No preservatives. Just real baking, done with care.

— Zoë Mokoena
✦

The Person Behind the Oven

Meet Zoë

Zoë Mokoena

Head Baker & Founder

Zoë has been baking professionally for over 12 years. Trained in classic French pastry technique but raised on her gogo's koeksisters and milk tart, her bakes blend the best of both worlds. Every cake that leaves this kitchen has been tasted, adjusted, and tasted again — nothing goes out unless it's perfect.

Frequently Asked Questions

For standard boxes of 6 or 12, we often have same-day availability — just WhatsApp us to check. Custom and themed orders need a minimum of 48 hours notice. Wedding and large event orders (50+ cupcakes) need at least 2 weeks notice.

Yes! We deliver across Johannesburg and surrounding areas. Delivery is quoted separately based on your location. Collection from our Roodepoort kitchen is always free. WhatsApp us your area for a delivery quote.

Yes — all our products are halaal certified. We use only halaal-certified ingredients and maintain a halaal kitchen. A certificate is available on request.

We offer gluten-free sponge as an option for any flavour at R32 per cupcake. Please mention your dietary requirements when ordering. We do not currently offer vegan options but are working on it!

The easiest way is to WhatsApp us on +27 76 206 1716. Tell us what you need, your date, and we'll confirm availability and take a 50% deposit to secure your order. Balance is due on collection or delivery.

We accept EFT (bank transfer) and cash. We do not currently accept card payments in person. A 50% deposit is required to confirm all orders, with the balance due before collection or delivery.
